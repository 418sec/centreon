------------------------------------------------
-    How to upgrade ODS 1.4 to ODS 1.4.1 ?
------------------------------------------------


1. Stop ODS

/etc/init.d/ods stop

2. make Oreon 1.4.1 upgrade

3. make ods database upgrade with howToUpgradeODS-1.4To1.4.1.sql

It may take a long time if your database is big. be patient !!

4. now you can start ods

/etc/init.d/ods start
